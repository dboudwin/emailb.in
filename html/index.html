emailb.in
  • SMTP to HTML sink for 1 or 10,000 emails

Welcome to emailb.in! To get started, send an email to any allowable address@emailb.in, then simply browse to http://address.emailb.in to see the raw email as it was received.

The last 3 emails sent to that address are available (fifo). Emails are cleared after 24 hours.

Remember, addresses here are not private! If you can read a sent email so can the rest of the internet :)
